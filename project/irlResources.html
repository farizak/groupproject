Meetups and Classes and Programs

  • Beginner Programmers

    Beginning Programmers is a Meetup based in Manhattan,New York. Beginner Programmers also encourages developers to join one of their many bootcamps. They range from Android mobile app development to Full Stack development.


  • JavaScript 101

    MakerSquare is a Javascript Meetup based in Manhattan, New York. They hold 101 classes, which encourages absolute beginners to dip their toes in learning variables, syntax, and making browsers interactive. They are also know for their Q&A sessions with founders. Members can sign up for free through meetup.com.


  • Learn Python

    Learn Python NYC is a Meetup based in Manhattan,New York that hosts a number of conferences, learning sessions, and weekly study groups regarding the Python programming language.


  • NYC Tech Talent Pipeline

    The NYC Tech Talent Pipeline strives to make native New Yorkers the center prospects for public and private tech companies. By working with a range of partners they commit to a solution that encourages hiring of New Yorkers. In addition to providing some guidance opportunities to succeed in the current job market.

  • Women Who Code

    The New York Chapter of Women Who Code inspires women to get into the field of technology, development, and computer science. The organization provides free classes world-wide. Women Who Code strives to further balance the portion of women in the tech workforce.


  • Web Designer Depot

    Web Designer Depot is a collaborative blog that outlines the developments of technology. They have news updates, free downloads, and apps.
